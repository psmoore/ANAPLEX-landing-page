We've spent decades modeling energy uncertainty.

Lumina Decision Systems brings deep expertise in utility financial modeling, uncertainty analysis, and regulatory decision support. Our team includes the architects of FINDER—the Financial Impacts of Distributed Energy Resources model developed at Lawrence Berkeley National Laboratory with U.S. Department of Energy funding.

FINDER was publicly vetted over more than a decade, peer-reviewed, and applied across multiple states to evaluate how emerging technologies affect utility shareholders and ratepayers. We've extended that foundation into ANAPLEX to address today's most pressing challenge: planning for AI-driven load growth under deep uncertainty.
